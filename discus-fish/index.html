Discus Fish are one of the most rewarding freshwater fish to take care of. Known for their vibrant colors, peaceful temperament & elegant demeanor they will surely win you over!

While Discus fish are endlessly captivating they come with a large amount of work to keep them healthy. Before starting I'm assuming that you know about the following topics below.

  • Understand The Nitrogen Cycle
  • Understand the Basics of water chemistry.
  • Understand how to dechlorinate your water
  • Understand how to keep your PH levels low and stable
  • Understand the importance of not over feeding your fish
  • Understand the importance of quarantining your Discus fish
  • Understand common diseases that spread among discuss fish

That being stated lets start off with some of the basics on how to keep your discuss fish healthy.
